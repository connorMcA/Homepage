About Me

I am currently a fourth year Computer Science student at Rochester Institute of Technology. One of my passions is helping others and programming gives me the opportunity to help many people. Whether that means developing tools and libraries for other developers to use or designing software that makes the end user's life a little simpler, I strive to make it easier for others to go after their own passions.

Another passion I have is for the arts. Art provides a way to express ideas and emotions in a way that language is sometimes incapable of. Personally I like exploring the art of patterns, repetition, and minimalism. The works of Philip Glass and Steve Reich have influenced me both as a musician and as a person. There can be just as much said with little statements as with big movements.
