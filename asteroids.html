Asteroids

This is a version of Asteroids that I made for one of my classes. It is almost identical to the version on my GitHub but that one was made in MonoGame and this was made in Unity. It varies from the normal Asteroids in that at the beginning you can choose a different fire mode for the ship. Currently I am working on adding a highscore feature to keep track of the top 10 scores. Feel free to email me with any suggestions or bugs!
